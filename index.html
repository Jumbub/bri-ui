About Me

Growing up in the arts, my passion for arts management began at an early age. From creative entrepreneurial projects as early as early as 8 years old, to large-scale project delivery through LGAs and NFPs in my professional career, working with the community to improve access to arts and culture has always been my biggest motivation within my varied roles in the creative industries.

Through Briony Rose Consulting, I am able to provide a passionate approach to problem solving, and delivering a variety of projects of different scales. I look forward to working with you on your next project!
